Exclam - Instructions

Coordinates... Why?

Every square on the board has a name (or 'coordinate'). Knowing these names makes it easy to communicate with your friends at the bar or at the library.

Learning the names is tedious and time consuming. Exclam! tries to make this more fun!

What do I do?

There are 2 types of games: 1) Tapping the correct squares; and 2) tapping the button with the correct coordinate.

When Tapping the correct square all you have to do is look at the name of the square that is shown in the middle of the board, and tap that square on the board.

When Tapping the correct button look at the board which square is lit up. Find the coordinate, and click the button with the coordinate on it.

In standard games you will have 30 seconds to give as many correct answers as you can. You can only make 2 mistakes per game to succeed, so don't go too fast.

It gets harder

As you progress, more of the board is used until at the entire board gets used at the same time.

Once you pass the final round, you should be skilled enough to follow all your favorite chess content creators.
